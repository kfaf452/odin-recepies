Shami Kabab

Serve in a burger bun, raita, or even by itself. Can't really go wrong. Enjoy!

Ingredients

  • 2 pounds ground beef
  • 1 tablespoon garlic paste
  • 1 tablespoon ginger paste
  • 1 teaspoon ground coriander
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground tumeric
  • 1/2 teaspoon garam masala
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground red chile pepper
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 1/2 cup chana dal
  • 1/2 cup chopped cliantro
  • salt to taste

Directions

  1. Heat a stockpot over medium heat; add ground beef. Stir in garlic paste, ginger paste, coriander, cumin, turmeric, garam masala, 1/2 teaspoon ground red chile, and salt to taste.
  2. Add water and dal; simmer until meat is fully cooked and all water has evaporated, 30 to 35 minutes.
  3. Blend beef and dal mixture together using an immersion blender in the stockpot. Add 1/2 cup cilantro; blend to combine.
  4. Roll spoonfuls of beef and dal mixture into into walnut-sized balls; shape into smooth flat patties.
  5. Whisk eggs, 1 tablespoon cilantro, 1/2 teaspoon ground red chile, and salt to taste together in a bowl. Dip each patty into egg mixture.
  6. Heat oil in a skillet over medium heat. Fry patties in a single layer until golden brown and crisp, 2 to 4 minutes per side.
