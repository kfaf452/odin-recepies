Sheer Khurma

A traditional dessert specially made for Eid festivals. No Eid breakfast is complete without this tasty and easy dish.

Ingredients

  • 1 quart milk
  • 5 tablespoon white sugar
  • 8 cardamom seeds
  • 8 ounce semiya
  • 8 raisins

Directions

  1. Bring milk, sugar, and cardamom seeds to a boil in a saucepan; reduce heat to medium-low and simmer for 5 minutes
  2. Stir broken vermicelli pasta and raisins into milk mixture and simmer until pasta is tender and the cream sauce has thickened, about 5 more minutes.
  3. Remove from heat and let stand until warm, about 15 minutes. If pudding is too thick, stir in a little milk.
